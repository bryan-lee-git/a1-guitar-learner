The "Why" Behind A1 Guitar Learner

Let's start with getting the name out of the way... We named ourselves "The Panda Warriors", totally off of a whim. We wanted to have fun with this project and we seemed to accomplish exactly that.

The A1 in "A1 Guitar Learner" stands for "All-in-one". We wanted to create one application that a user could go to in order to find everything that they need to begin playing the guitar!

The four of us started this project for a Coding and Web Development course that we were taking through the University of Utah. It has been a really fun learning experience and a great way to immerse ourselves into the world of Web Development.

One thing that we all seemed to have in common was our love of music and our innate desire to help others find their love for it as well. We decided to build off of that and create something that was easy to use, fun, and useful on the go. Hopefully this helps tons of people across the globe begin their journey of learning how to play the guitar!

Credit Where Credit Is Due!

We would like to extend credit to the other great developers that built some of the amazing things that we implemented into our application.
